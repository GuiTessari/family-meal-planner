# Product Vision

## Overview

Family Meal Planner is a platform designed to help families organize their meals, prepare weekly meal plans, track eating habits, and build healthier routines together.

The product combines meal planning, shopping lists, meal preparation workflows, and AI-powered suggestions to reduce the effort required to maintain a healthy diet.

The system is intended to work as a co-pilot rather than a decision maker. AI enhances the experience but is not required for the core functionality.

---

# Problem Statement

Many families struggle to maintain healthy eating habits because meal planning requires a significant amount of time and coordination.

Common challenges include:

- Diet plans are stored in PDFs and are difficult to manage.
- Family members have different goals, restrictions, and preferences.
- Weekly meal preparation is time-consuming.
- Shopping lists are created manually.
- Healthy habits are hard to track consistently.
- Feedback about meals is lost over time.
- Existing tools focus on individuals instead of families.

As a result, meal planning becomes stressful and inconsistent.

---

# Target Audience

## Primary Audience

Families that want to improve their eating habits and organize meal preparation.

Examples:

- Parents managing their children's nutrition.
- Couples following different diet plans.
- Families preparing meals in batches.

---

# Product Goals

The platform should allow users to:

- Centralize family diet plans.
- Plan weekly meals.
- Generate shopping lists automatically.
- Organize meal preparation sessions.
- Track meal completion and habits.
- Collect feedback on recipes and meals.
- Learn from historical data.
- Improve future recommendations.

---

# Core Principles

## Family-first

The product is designed around families instead of individuals.

## Simplicity

The application must be easy to use, especially on a tablet placed in the kitchen.

## Incremental Improvement

Users should receive value even without AI features.

## AI as an Assistant

Artificial intelligence should support users, not replace decision making.

## Long-term Learning

The system should improve its suggestions based on habits and feedback.

---

# Platforms

## Web Application

Used for administrative tasks:

- Managing family members.
- Uploading diet plans.
- Configuring preferences.
- Reviewing history.
- Managing recipes.
- Planning meals.

## Kitchen Tablet

Used for daily interactions:

- Viewing meals.
- Tracking habits.
- Following recipes.
- Managing meal preparation.
- Reviewing shopping lists.

---

# Success Metrics

The product will be considered successful if it helps families:

- Increase diet consistency.
- Reduce time spent planning meals.
- Reduce food waste.
- Improve meal satisfaction.
- Maintain healthier routines over time.

---

# Out of Scope (Initial Version)

The first version will not include:

- Social features.
- Multi-family support.
- Calendar integrations.
- Grocery delivery integrations.
- Native mobile applications.
- Advanced notifications.
- Automatic nutrition recommendations.

These features may be explored in future versions.

---

# Long-term Vision

Family Meal Planner aims to become the central hub for family nutrition and meal organization, combining structured planning, habit tracking, and intelligent assistance in a single platform.
