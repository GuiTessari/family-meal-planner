# MVP Scope

## Purpose

The goal of the MVP is to help a family organize weekly meal preparation based on individual diets.

The system should transform multiple diet plans into a practical weekly meal plan, helping users decide what to cook, how much to prepare, and how to assemble meals for each family member.

The MVP should prioritize simplicity and solve the core operational problem before introducing automation, analytics, or AI-driven features.

---

# Problem Statement

Managing multiple diets within the same household is time-consuming and difficult.

Each family member may have different nutritional requirements, meal schedules, and portion sizes. Turning those individual diets into a practical weekly cooking routine requires manual calculations and planning.

The MVP aims to simplify this process by generating a weekly meal plan and supporting meal preparation.

---

# Goals

The MVP must allow users to:

* Manage family members.
* Create or import individual diets.
* Define meals and portion sizes.
* Generate a weekly meal plan.
* Review and approve the generated plan.
* Generate cooking instructions for the week.
* Generate meal assembly instructions for each family member.
* Export the resulting plan.

---

# Inputs

The system receives:

* Family members.
* Individual diets.
* Meal definitions.
* Ingredients.
* Recipes.

---

# Outputs

The system generates:

## Weekly Meal Plan

A complete view of what each family member will eat during the week.

Example:

* Monday breakfast.
* Tuesday lunch.
* Wednesday dinner.

---

## Cooking Plan

A list of everything that should be prepared in advance.

Example:

* Cook 2 kg of chicken breast.
* Prepare 1 kg of rice.
* Roast vegetables.

---

## Cooking Instructions

Step-by-step instructions describing how to prepare the recipes required for the week.

---

## Meal Assembly Instructions

Instructions describing how prepared food should be divided among family members.

Example:

* Guilherme: 110 g rice + 65 g beans + 120 g chicken.
* Karol: 70 g rice + 50 g beans + 80 g chicken.

---

# Out of Scope

The following features are intentionally excluded from the MVP:

* Shopping lists.
* Habit tracking.
* Dashboards and analytics.
* Notifications.
* Calendar integrations.
* Mobile applications.
* Multi-family support.
* AI-generated meal suggestions.
* Meal feedback collection.
* Nutrition recommendations.
* Voice interactions.

---

# Future Vision

Future versions of the product may include:

* Smart meal suggestions.
* Recipe transformations.
* Alternative meal options.
* Shopping list generation.
* Habit tracking.
* Meal feedback.
* AI-powered insights.
* Native mobile applications.
* Automation and integrations.

---

# Success Criteria

The MVP will be considered successful if it allows a family to:

1. Import or create their diets.
2. Generate a weekly meal plan.
3. Decide what to cook for the week.
4. Follow the generated instructions.
5. Organize meal preparation with minimal manual effort.
